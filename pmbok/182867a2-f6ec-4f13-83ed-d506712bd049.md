# R12 — Switch cache exposure

Original design placed switch cache inside vault (`<vault>/.exocortex/switch-cache/`). При vault portability (cp -R к external drive) — cached AS contents leak в backup. При Obsidian Sync — cached contents distributed к all devices independently of profile config.

Mitigation: cache location outside vault per platform conventions. Inherently not part of vault state.
